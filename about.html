About Estidama

International Services Within Local Reach

Estidama International Consultants Ltd is an interdisciplinary firm delivering specialist green building, sustainability, commissioning and project management services. We are small enough to give personal attention, and large enough to handle complex programmes.

Founded with a commitment to driving measurable environmental impact, we bring together accredited professionals across green building certification, building science, commissioning and project management to deliver integrated, end-to-end solutions.

From LEED and WELL certifications in East Africa to net zero carbon strategies for global portfolios, we consistently deliver international-standard outcomes — guided by our founding belief that "international services can and should be accessible within local reach."

"To harness our capabilities to springboard long-term sustainable solutions to project stakeholders and the environment."
